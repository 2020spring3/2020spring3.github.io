Copyright Scavenger Hunt Answer Key

By: Student #3 Web Design Spring 2020

  1. What is fair use?

    Fair use allows a resource to be used but other people but it protects it from other people claiming that it is theirs.

  2. What can happen if you violate copyright laws?

    The situation will go to court and the infringer can have to pay between $200 and $150,000, and they could go to jail.

  3. What is a public domain?

    A domain that is not protected by copyright.

  4. What are Terms of Service?

    The rules a someone must follow if they would like to use a product.

  5. Anything written before the year ___ is considered to be in a public domain and why?

    Anything made before 1923 is considered in a public domain because the author of the content is probably not alive to claim it.
