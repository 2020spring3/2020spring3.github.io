Steps to Shoot a Bow: Using the Jigsaw Method

By: Student #3 Web Design Spring 2020

Directions:
  1. Break into groups of 5.
  2. Decide who in each group would like to research which two topics below.
  3. Research your chosen topic.
  4. Get back into your groups once everyone is finished with step 3.
  5. Each person in the group will present what they learned while the people listening will take notes on what they learned from everyone's presentations.
